VOLUNTEER AT NORTHVIEW

Serving at Northview is a great way to use your gifts and get to know others! We encourage everyone to be a part of a Team. Serving is a huge part of who we are, and we believe that serving can help us grow spiritually.

What do you do?

These are the folks who make the Weekend Services happen! From camera operators and guitarists, to those with a more administrative bent in a directing role, there’s a whole crew of people who work hard to make the weekends great!
